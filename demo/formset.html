Email Addresses

This email addresses section is DynamoForm powered. Click the Add Email button to add more email address lines dynamically. Click the Delete button to remove an address line dynamically. You can optionally configure the minimum and maximum number of form elements you would like to allow. This form requires at least 1 email address, but no more than 4 are accepted.

Here is the HTML for the above example

<div class="dynamo-formset"  data-min-rows="1" data-max-rows="4">
  <div class="dynamo-formset-row">
    <label for="email_1" data-dynamo-relabel="true">Email 1</label>
    <input type="email" id="email_1" name="email_1">
    <button type="button" class="dynamo-formset-row-delete" disabled="disabled">Delete</button>
  </div>
  <button type="button" class="dynamo-formset-row-add">Add Email</button>
</div>
